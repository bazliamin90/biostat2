Tailed test

  1. One-tailed test Two-tailed test
    Researcher wants to know about differences that occur:
    in one direction from mean.
    • is A > B? (vice versa)
    		 in both direction from mean.
    Example
    in a study,
    • blood samples of students were taken.
    • mean serum cholesterol level of them were recorded.
    • next, they were given high-fibre diet for three years.
    • semi-annual blood sampling to measure decrease in mean cholesterol.
    		 new antidepressant drug was given to a group of MDD patients.
    • after 6 months, how many showed beneficial effects, as opposed to deleterious?
    • the researcher interested to know both improvements AND detrimental effects of the drugs.
