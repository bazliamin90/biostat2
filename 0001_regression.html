=====◈◈◈◈◈◈◈◈◈◈=====
  1. To estimate association between independent and dependent variable.
  2. Independent variable (iv) and dependent variable (dv) can be continuous or categorical (binary).[1]
  3. Some references [2] stated that the iv must be a continuous variable.
  4. How to choose:
    1. iv + continuous dv = linear regression.
    2. iv + categorical dv = logistic regression.
  5. Example:
    • iv: Cancer, copd , ild
    • dv: Days of stay at ICU (cont.), palliative care present or absent (cat.).
  6. In the above example, both linear and logistic regression will be used.
  7. Regression tests, simplified: [2]
