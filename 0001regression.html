Linear & logistic regression

  1. Estimate association between independent and dependent variable.
  2. Independent variable (iv) and dependent variable (dv) can be continuous or categorical.[1]
  3. iv + continuous dv = linear regression.
  4. iv + categorical dv = logistic regression.
  5. Example:
    iv: Cancer, copd , ild
    dv: Days of stay at ICU (cont.), palliative care present or absent (cat.).
  6. In the above example, both linear and logistic regression will be used.
